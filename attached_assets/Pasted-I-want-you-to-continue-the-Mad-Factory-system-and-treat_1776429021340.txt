## Monthly closing process
I want a clear monthly closing workflow.
That means the system should support a process like:
1. choose a month or date range
2. gather all relevant financial data
3. calculate totals
4. calculate profit or loss
5. store a closing record if needed
6. use that closing for shareholder distribution and historical reporting

The monthly closing must be traceable.
Do not make it a mysterious calculation that changes randomly.
If a closing is saved, it should behave like a real historical record.
